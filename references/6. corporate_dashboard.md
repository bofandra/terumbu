# Next Page: Corporate Impact & ESG Dashboard

The next page should be the **Corporate Impact Dashboard**, because it represents Terumbu.eco’s strongest B2B revenue opportunity.

It allows companies to fund conservation programs, engage employees, monitor project implementation, collect verified evidence, and generate credible CSR or ESG reports.

The experience should combine:

* An executive ESG dashboard
* A conservation project-monitoring system
* An employee engagement portal
* A financial utilization report
* An evidence and reporting center

The first mockup should use a fictional company:

> **Nusantara Bank — Ocean Impact Program 2026**

---

# 1. Page Objectives

## Primary objective

Give corporate stakeholders a clear view of how company funding is being used and what conservation activities have been delivered.

## Secondary objectives

* Monitor funded campaigns and projects
* Review budget utilization
* Track output and outcome indicators
* Manage employee participation
* Download ESG and CSR reports
* Access supporting evidence
* Compare progress against company targets
* Publish a branded public impact page
* Identify delayed or at-risk projects
* Approve new corporate programs

## Main user journey

```text
Review portfolio
→ Check progress and risks
→ Inspect project evidence
→ Review employee participation
→ Generate report
→ Decide the next funding action
```

---

# 2. Corporate User Roles

The dashboard should support role-based access.

## Executive Viewer

* CEO

Can:

* Manage programs

* Manage corporate targets
* Export data

* Review committed and disbursed funds

* Export financial reports

## Employee Engagement Manager

* Manage employee registration
* Review participation

* Approved evidence

# 12. Project Health Status

Use standardized states.

## On Track

Work and spending are progressing according to plan.

## Needs Attention

Minor schedule, reporting, or evidence issues exist.

## At Risk

Important milestone, financial, or delivery problems require action.

## Awaiting Verification

Partner has submitted evidence that is still being reviewed.

## Completed

Final activities and reporting have been approved.

## Suspended

Funding or implementation has been temporarily stopped.

Each status must include an explanation, not only a color.

# 49. Security and Governance

Corporate data requires stronger controls.

## Required controls

* Role-based access
* Multi-factor authentication
* Encryption in transit and at rest
* Approval workflows
* Immutable audit logs
* Document versioning
* Data-retention settings
* Session timeout
* Export logging
* IP or domain restrictions for enterprise clients

## Sensitive data

Employee-level donation amounts, medical information, and expedition documents must not appear in executive dashboards unless explicitly authorized.
