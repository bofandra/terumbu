# Next Page: Conservation Expedition Detail

This page converts destination interest into a trusted booking while showing that the trip creates measurable conservation impact.

The first design should use:

> **Raja Ampat Coral Restoration Expedition**
> 4 days / 3 nights · Raja Ampat · Coral planting and reef monitoring

The page should feel like a combination of a premium travel marketplace, conservation project page, and field-program briefing.

---

# 1. Page Objectives

## Primary objective

Convert visitors into confirmed expedition participants.

## Secondary objectives

* Explain the conservation activity clearly
* Establish operator and safety credibility
* Show how the trip supports local communities
* Help users determine whether the trip suits their abilities
* Answer logistical questions before checkout
* Connect the expedition to campaigns, courses, and the Impact Passport

## User decision flow

```text
Discover destination
→ Understand the experience
→ Verify dates, price, and suitability
→ Review conservation impact
→ Check itinerary and inclusions
→ Select participants
→ Book securely
```

* Keyboard-accessible booking card
