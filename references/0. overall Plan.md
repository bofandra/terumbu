The single most important differentiator is not crowdfunding or tourism. It is the **"Digital Impact Passport"**—every user accumulates a lifelong record of donations, expeditions, learning, volunteering, and ecosystem restoration. That personal impact identity is what will keep users engaged for years rather than making a one-time donation and leaving.
